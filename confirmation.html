What to do next

1

Print your will

Download the PDF from your email and print it single-sided on standard A4 paper. Do not staple the pages together yet.

2

Sign with two witnesses

Gather two witnesses over the age of 18 who are not beneficiaries named in your will. You must all be in the same room. Sign the final page and initial the bottom of every other page. Your witnesses must do the same.

3

Store safely

Keep your original signed will in a safe, fireproof place. Let your appointed Executor know exactly where to find it. You can also file it with the Israeli Inheritance Registrar for an additional government fee.
