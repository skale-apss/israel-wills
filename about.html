Why I started IsraelWills

After a decade of practicing estate law in Tel Aviv, I saw a recurring pattern. Hardworking Israelis were putting off making a will because they thought it was too expensive, too complicated, or simply too morbid to think about.

But when the unexpected happens, not having a will leaves families in turmoil. The Israeli inheritance laws take over, freezing bank accounts and distributing assets according to rigid state formulas rather than your personal wishes.

I built IsraelWills.com to solve this. Utilizing my legal expertise, I created a platform that makes drafting a legally-binding Israeli will as simple as ordering groceries online—combining expert legal backing with modern technology to provide ultimate peace of mind at a fraction of the traditional cost.
